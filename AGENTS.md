# AI Contribution Guidelines for fuck-your-charges

## Purpose
This document outlines the contribution guidelines specific to AI initiatives within the `fuck-your-charges` project.

## General Guidelines
1. **Contribution Clarity**: Each AI contribution should be clearly documented, including the problem it addresses and its expected impact.
2. **Testing**: Every AI enhancement must come with unit tests to ensure functionality and prevent regressions.
3. **Code Quality**: Follow the established code quality standards of the project. Use meaningful variable names, and write self-explanatory code.
4. **Documentation**: Contributions must be accompanied by updated documentation, explaining the AI components in detail.
5. **Performance Considerations**: Be mindful of performance. Contributions should be efficient and scalable.

## Specific AI Component Contributions
- Provide clear descriptions of your models and algorithms used.
- Explain the reasoning behind the selection of any pre-trained models, datasets, or libraries included in your contributions.

## Review Process
All contributions will be reviewed during the pull request process and must meet project-level quality standards.
